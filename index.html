What is Abiotic
Factor?

Abiotic Factor is a survival-crafting game released in July 2025. Set in the depths of the GATE Cascade facility, an underground research facility in the middle of the Australian desert studying anomalous phenomena, your first day as a scientist is disrupted by an otherworldly containment breach and a military incursion. This game draws influence from many popular Sci-Fi horror, namely the SCP Foundation and Half-Life series of games.
